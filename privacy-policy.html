2. What Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Consultation Forms: Name, email, phone number, fitness goals, health information
  • Membership Applications: Personal details, emergency contacts, payment information
  • Communications: Messages, emails, phone calls, and feedback you send us

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Website Usage: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent on site
  • Cookies and Tracking: Google Analytics data, Facebook Pixel data
  • Payment Information: Transaction details (processed securely by our payment providers)

2.3 Special Category Data

We may collect health-related information necessary for providing safe fitness services, including:

  • Medical conditions or injuries
  • Fitness assessments and progress tracking
  • Dietary preferences or restrictions

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Consent: For marketing communications and non-essential cookies
  • Contract: To provide our fitness services and process payments
  • Legitimate Interests: To improve our services and website functionality
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with health and safety regulations

3.2 Purposes of Processing

  • Providing fitness coaching and personal training services
  • Processing payments and managing memberships
  • Communicating about classes, schedules, and updates
  • Marketing our services (with your consent)
  • Improving our website and services
  • Complying with legal obligations

4. Who We Share Your Information With

4.1 Service Providers

We share data with trusted third parties who help us operate:

  • GoHighLevel (CRM): Customer relationship management and communications
  • GoTeamUp: Class booking and schedule management
  • Stripe & GoCardless: Secure payment processing
  • Email Marketing Platforms: For newsletters and promotional emails (with consent)
  • Google Analytics: Website performance and user behavior analysis
  • Meta (Facebook): Advertising and marketing analytics

4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or to protect our legitimate interests.

4.3 International Transfers

Some of our service providers are located outside the UK/EU. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for any international data transfers, including:

  • Adequacy decisions by the UK/EU authorities
  • Standard Contractual Clauses
  • Certification schemes where applicable

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

5.1 Essential Cookies

We use essential cookies to make our website function properly. These cannot be disabled.

5.2 Analytics and Marketing Cookies

  • Google Analytics: Tracks website usage and performance
  • Facebook Pixel: Enables targeted advertising and measures ad effectiveness
  • Marketing Cookies: Help us show you relevant advertisements

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings or our cookie banner.

5.3 Facebook/Meta Data Usage

When you interact with our Facebook ads or website, Meta may collect information including:

  • Website visits and page views
  • Form submissions and conversions
  • Device and browser information

This data is used to:

  • Show you relevant advertisements
  • Measure advertising effectiveness
  • Create lookalike audiences for marketing

For more information, see Meta's Privacy Policy.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for different periods depending on the type:

  • Customer Records: 7 years after last interaction (legal requirement)
  • Financial Records: 7 years (tax and accounting requirements)
  • Marketing Data: Until you unsubscribe or withdraw consent
  • Website Analytics: 26 months (Google Analytics default)
  • CCTV Footage: 30 days (if applicable)

7. Your Rights Under GDPR

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

7.1 Right of Access

Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

7.2 Right to Rectification

Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.

7.3 Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")

Request deletion of your personal data in certain circumstances.

7.4 Right to Restrict Processing

Ask us to limit how we use your data.

7.5 Right to Data Portability

Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

7.6 Right to Object

Object to processing based on legitimate interests, including direct marketing.

7.7 Rights Related to Automated Decision Making

We do not use automated decision-making or profiling that significantly affects you.

7.8 How to Exercise Your Rights

Contact us at sam@atlas-gyms.co.uk or 07490 253471. We will respond within one month.

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access Controls: Limited access on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular Security Updates: Our systems are regularly updated
  • Staff Training: Team members receive data protection training
  • Secure Payment Processing: We use PCI-compliant payment providers
